- What is statistical bias? ; A systematic error where a sample is unrepresentative in a consistent direction. ; [Biases: An Introduction]
- What is cognitive bias? ; A systematic error in thinking that distorts reality and is not always corrected by more data. ; [Biases: An Introduction]
- What is base rate neglect? ; Judging based on how characteristics fit together while neglecting their commonality in the population. ; [Biases: An Introduction]
- What is the sunk cost fallacy? ; The tendency to commit to past investments instead of cutting losses. ; [Biases: An Introduction]
- What is bias blind spot? ; The tendency to recognize biases in others but not in ourselves. ; [Biases: An Introduction]
